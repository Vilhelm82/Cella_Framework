## LEAD-1 — The κ_{r;p,q} grid at n ≥ 4 ("Role-Channel Curvature II")

**Status:** OPEN — the source document's own named best-shot for original mathematics
(recurrence, vanishing, Lorentzian signature, Pascal-like structure in the mixed
bidegrees).
**What changed since 2026-06-26:** three edges of the grid are now mapped — the
pure-coupling column (depth theorem: κ_{r;r,0} realizes Specht S^(n−r,r), proven all
r, n ≥ 2r), the r = 2 row at general n (triangle/shadow law: Johnson operator × one
scalar), and the normalization boundary (parity law). The unmapped interior is the
mixed-bidegree region; "minimal coupling degree p to reach depth r" is open.
**Discriminating test:** exhaust the grid exactly at n = 4, 5 for r = 2, 3 via the
two-variable Vandermonde extraction (the RC-1 harness pattern generalizes directly);
sweep for recurrences across (p,q), forced vanishings, and the signature of the
channel quadratic form (the n=3 Lorentzian (2,1) as the base case).
**Note:** natural first Cella discovery-loop campaign after Layer 1 opens.

## LEAD-2 — Role singularities (the stratum classification theorem)

**Status:** OPEN — vocabulary exists, theorem does not. Layer 0 refusals plus A-008's
tokens (`ROLE_CHART_UNAVAILABLE`, `CHANNEL_ISOTROPIC`, both strata witnessed in RC-4)
let the engine *refuse* at degeneracies; nothing yet lets it *read* them.
**The question:** connect the algebraic failures (a=0/b=0, Δ_n=0, det S=0, κ_i=κ_j,
tangent contact, cone apex) to what happens geometrically to the channel account and
the role orbit across the boundary — stratum transitions as diagnostics, not errors.
**Discriminating test:** one-parameter families crossing each stratum, exact-ℚ channel
accounts on both sides plus the blow-up rate; does each stratum type have a
characteristic channel signature? (Physics-adjacent payoff: folds, caustics,
horizons, phase boundaries — after the mathematics earns it.)

## LEAD-3 — Channel topology (coupling-graph classification)

**Status:** OPEN, tractability transformed by the triangle law.
**The question:** classify channel spectra by Hessian coupling-graph topology (no
edge / single edge / chain / triangle / star / bipartite / complete); generalize the
proven single-edge pinning law to rank-k edge sets.
**What changed:** the triangle decomposition expresses the general-n coupling channel
as a T_S-weighted sum over vertex-triangles — the combinatorial machinery this lead
needs already exists and is certified adjacent (I.3b lineage; RC-1's edge-vector form).
**Discriminating test:** for each graph class at n = 4, 5, compute the exact channel
spectrum and the gauge-rigid (null) directions; check whether topology class ⟹
spectrum signature is injective on the swept families.

## LEAD-4 — The odd sector: √q diagnostics

**Status:** OPEN — **and newly unblocked by G0.3.** This lead was parked partly on
tooling: no exact carrier for the radical sector. `QSqrt` is implemented and
gate-certified (14/14 ×2), so odd-order invariants are now exactly computable in the
engine for the first time. Cost dropped from "build infrastructure, then investigate"
to "investigate."
**The question:** is the odd tier (σ_odd ∈ ℚ·√q — extrinsic, bending-sensitive) a
useful diagnostic layer distinct from the rational even tier? Includes the adversarial
role: the odd sector is the derived defence to the isometric-bending attack.
**Discriminating test:** compute σ₁ (and σ₃ at n=5) exactly across a bending family
that holds all even invariants fixed; certify that the odd tier separates what the
even tier provably cannot.

## LEAD-5 — Order-3 transport and the role groupoid proper

**Status:** OPEN. Third-order rational transport formulas exist as paper raw material;
nothing at order 3 is certified in-repo. The generalization from the S₃ action to a
genuine local groupoid of output charts (and to n > 3 roles) is untouched.
**Discriminating test:** re-derive and certify the order-3 recharting law at the
keystone (RC-5 candidate); check whether the order-2 attribution theorem (Stage B
form) survives one order up — if the cross-term stays closed-form at order 3, the
groupoid formalization is earned; if not, the boundary is the finding.

## LEAD-6 — D_static re-derived (carrier choice as a geometric observable)

**Status:** OPEN — reverted. The decidable structural factorability predicate over an
operation-DAG was internally rated the one genuinely novel computational item, but its
evidence base was the WARP and reverted to OPEN with it. That makes this a

**The question:** is membership in the factoring class decidable in advance from the

**Discriminating test:** define the predicate fresh over Layer-0 observation-map
chains; prove the biconditional on the rational-op class with an independent referee;
locate the wall (transcendental boundary) honestly.
