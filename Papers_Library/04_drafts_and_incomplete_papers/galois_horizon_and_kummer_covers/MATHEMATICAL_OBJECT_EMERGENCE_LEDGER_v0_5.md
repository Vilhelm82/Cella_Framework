## 3. Candidate card — CAND-001

**Descriptor:** cover–chamber descent system (provisional, not a permanent name).

**The recurring structure:** a finite algebraic cover + deck/monodromy action + distinguished real/hyperbolic chamber + distinguished physical branch + invariant/covariant thermodynamic data + norm-defined wall divisor + solvability obstruction. No single parent theory retains all of these at once.

**Data tuple (under revision — see NS-001):**

```text
T = (B, pi:X->B, G, C, s_phys, Omega, I, W)
```

The precision-flow attempt showed this tuple conflates two things the founding family had glued together: a **state cover** and an **output/selection map**. The revised working form separates them:

```text
T = (B, pi:X->B, p:Y->B, G, C, R:X->Y, Omega, I, W)
```

where `X` is the oriented state/account cover, `Y` an admissible-output object, `R:X->Y` the distinguished (possibly wall-decorated/partial) output map, and the founding "physical section" is the special case `Y = X`. Not yet enacted as the permanent definition — must be tested back against the Kerr–Newman and axial-horizon realizations first.

**The essential datum (what every serious reduction discards):** the physical *interpretation* of the branch selection — which chamber/ray is physical, what the selected branch means thermodynamically, the BPS meaning of the walls, and the coupling to the covariant/reconstruction package. The selection *mechanism* is spectral and absorbable; the selection *meaning* is not. Program thesis, in current sharpened form: **"the selection is spectral; the physics is the interpretation of the selection."**

## 11. Standing method

```text
- Promote in order (coincidence -> ... -> autonomous field); numerical or
  structural agreement is evidence, never promotion by enthusiasm.
- Record a remainder only if >=3 parents discard it.
- A native theorem must not be an imported theorem with renamed symbols.
- Autonomy requires a nonrepresentable example (none yet) — an object
  satisfying the axioms that arises from no founding realization.
- Never erase a refuted row; change its status and link the correction.
- Excitement chooses the next experiment; it gets no vote at the promotion
  gate.
```
