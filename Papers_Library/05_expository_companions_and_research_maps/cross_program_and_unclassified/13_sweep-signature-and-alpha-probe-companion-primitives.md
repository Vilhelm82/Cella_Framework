## 1. What the arc set out to build

Two pre-registered Codex tasks, each with a full pre-reg committed before implementation (the pre-regs themselves state "Pre-registration committed prior to implementation"):

**Task 035 — `sweep_signature_probe` (a new Layer-1 primitive).** Goal (codex_task035 L23-25): introduce a typed Layer-1 primitive that "emits a typed `SweepSignature` evidencing how an opaque scalar observable behaves under a finite-precision sweep — specifically, whether the observable's chain shows cancellation dominance, tail-cell substrate refusal, degenerate-input behaviour, or insufficient data." It is built by composing two existing L1 primitives — `typed_finite_difference` and `typed_log_log_slope` — into a single screening verdict (`SweepSignatureValue`) carrying seven `SweepSignatureStatus` strata. Its declared design source (L19) is a scratch `burden_sensor.py` v2.1 (calibrated 15/16 on Schwarzschild photon-sphere ground truth); the spec is explicit that the scratch is "the design source … not lifted into `src/` as-is" — signal formulas are re-derived from V4 primitives' exposed fields (`cancellation_grade = 1 - r_squared`, DP11 L39). Declared screening philosophy (DP10, L38): it is a **deliberately low-sensitivity pre-screen** — borderline cases are accepted as clean and routed to the heavier `directional_alpha_probe`; "the false-negative rate on borderline cases is a feature, not a defect." Three `Final` thresholds (`CANCELLATION_GRADE_THRESHOLD=0.70`, `DROP_RATE_THRESHOLD=0.30`, `MIN_OBSERVATIONS_FOR_FIT=3`) are calibration-justified against the 16 GR photon-sphere cases (8 stable × 8 naive). Source: `codex_task035_sweep_signature_probe.md` (read in full), `task035_sweep_signature_probe_summary.md` (read in full). _(high)_

**Task 036 — alpha-probe / sweep-signature companion-call pattern.** Goal (codex_task036 L23-25): extend `directional_alpha_probe` to **optionally** invoke `sweep_signature_probe` as a sibling companion call on the identical grid, attaching three new optional `None`-default fields to `AlphaProbeObservation` (`companion_sweep_signature_observation/_status/_trace_id`). The alpha-probe's primary `AlphaProbeStatus` verdict is unchanged; the companion fields are informational evidence letting a downstream consumer disambiguate `alpha_unstable_window` into "formulation-burdened" vs "structurally unstable" (DP3, L31; STATUS_CALCULUS four canonical patterns, L76-81). Caller-opt-in only (`companion_sweep_signature=False` default is bit-identical to pre-task036 output, DP1-2). One-way composition (alpha→sweep, never the reverse, to avoid circular composition; DP8 L36). No new status cells, no new transition rules — consumer conventions only. The stated purpose (L25) is to "establish the integration pattern that tasks 037 and 038 will follow." Source: `codex_task036_alpha_probe_sweep_signature_companion.md` (read in full), `task036_..._summary.md` (read in full). _(high)_

Both pre-regs frame this as a deliberate **return to Layer-1 primitive construction** after a long run of eval-layer/discipline/Vera arcs (Arcs 3-12). The work is explicitly tooling: a finer-grained screening instrument at the primitive layer.
