## 0. Charter

**Meta-thesis (one sentence):** the coupling/account primitive is a
property of the *act of representation*, not of floats and not of the
object's variables — and it is characterisable along two independent
axes: its **geometry** (coupling is the metric-free holonomy of the
residue account; the boundaries are two distinct primitives) and its
**algebra** (the residue composition factors, and the ⊕-algebra is
universal across domains).

**Geometry-arm thesis:** the coupling between two refinement axes is
the **loop-holonomy of the residue account** — the mixed second
difference of the exact residue — computable from the per-axis
refinement laws alone (a connection, no metric); exactly zero when the
axes are additively separable; exactly nonzero and exactly accountable
in ℚ when coupled (reading gap == account gap); separable from the
arithmetic rounding fingerprint in finite precision under the
protection predicate; and bounded below by a detection floor F that is
a *distinct* boundary primitive from coupling-degeneracy.

**Algebra-arm thesis (sibling, content largely in the reconciled
WARP draft):** a domain inherits account coherence iff it factors into
composable observation maps with exactly-representable composable
residues (factorability, Q1); and the residue ⊕-algebra is either
universal, per-domain, or graded across domains (⊕-universality, Q2).

**Relationship between arms:** independent machinery, shared
meta-thesis. Recommended execution: **two campaigns under one charter**
(§6, ruling #1) — the "run both" resolution in its native form. The
geometry arm is the one this brief specs; the algebra arm folds in the
existing WARP material once reconciled against TASK_TEMPLATE.

**Strategic placement:** the geometry arm is the load-bearing evidence
for the cella-as-trunk re-founding — but it does **not** confirm the
spine (§5, fenced). The exploratory probe `holonomy_stress.py` is its
Stage −1 evidence, not a pinned result; Stage A re-runs it as a frozen
battery with exact-ℚ predictions pinned before the harness.

## 2. Geometry arm — fixture classes (manifest to be frozen as FX-G)

- **FX-G-A (holonomy lanes, exact ℚ):** additively-separable controls
  (x+y) vs coupled (x·y, 1/(x+y)) on **two-encoding** grids (dyadic ×
  continued-fraction — two genuinely different encodings of one real,
  not two variables). The probe's battery, lifted and pinned.
- **FX-G-B (finite-precision separation):** the FX-G-A coupled
  fixtures in declared finite precision, with **protection-predicate-
  satisfied** cells (coupling present, rounding provably absent) vs
  **rounding** cells. The keystone discriminator: coupling-holonomy vs
  route fingerprint.
- **FX-G-C (representation-exhaustion boundary):** a coupled fixture
  deepened along one axis until the coupling crosses below the pinned
  floor F; certify-above / refuse-below cells; the boundary located.
- **FX-G-D (symbolic axis + Richardson):** fixtures carrying a symbolic
  refinement axis — decidable symbolic identities vs the
  Richardson-undecidable class (sin²+cos²−1 ≡ 0). Tests refusal
  localisation to the symbolic coordinate.
- **FX-G-E (three-account + non-archimedean):** three-axis fixtures
  where the balance reconciliation is measured; **at least one
  non-archimedean encoding** (2-adic) — the only fixture that actually
  tests "encoding-agnostic" rather than "agreed across two archimedean
  encodings."
- **FX-G-F (boundary inhabitation, Q3):** fixtures spanning the
  inhabitation tiers (sharp / conservative / un-inhabitable) and a
  **composed-gate** fixture (the conical blind spot — coupling-
  degeneracy ∘ representation-exhaustion — the first measured
  composed-gate failure).

  cross-term in ℚ (reading gap ≠ account gap). Exact accountability
  broken.

  rounding fingerprint stay entangled even under protection. The

- **K-G5 (descope, pre-declared):** exact/high-precision bit growth

## 5. Fences (explicitly OUT)

- **THE SPINE CLAIM IS FENCED.** This arm *measures* coupling-holonomy
  and boundary behaviour on named fixtures. It carries **no** claim that
  the coupling primitive is the universal substrate spine, and **no**
  cross-domain G-claim. "Encoding-agnostic" is a **navigation target**
  the fixtures test (FX-G-E specifically), not an asserted invariant.
  `[INFERENCE]` stays `[INFERENCE]`. *(The structural analog of Arm M's
  T2 fence; law in every prereg of this arm.)*
- **The spine is confirmed only at the charter level, only if BOTH arms
  survive.** This arm alone does not confirm it; neither does the
  algebra arm.
- **"The metric is unnecessary" is a framing, not a theorem this arm
  proves.** The arm measures the holonomy (which provably needs only the
  connection). It does not assert a metaphysical no-metric result.
- n ≥ 3 refinement axes beyond unscored smoke; the full adelic /
  product-formula structure beyond CL-G6's registered measurement;
  performance claims (covenant 3, forever).
